## Content approach (verified from blog review, 2026-05-22)

- Practical, small-business-focused content
- Heavy emphasis on SEO, local SEO, Google Business Profile, paid ads, website optimisation
- Question-based and actionable headlines ("How to...", "What Is...", "Complete Guide to...")
- Emphasis on realistic expectations and transparency about industry pitfalls
- "Measure everything. Optimise often" as a core philosophy
- "Failing fast" and A/B testing language used
