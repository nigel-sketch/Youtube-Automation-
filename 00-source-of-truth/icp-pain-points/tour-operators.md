# ICP: Tour Operators

**Status: STUB — sourced from Brand_Context.md only. Colin's full ICP file will replace/supplement this when shared.**

---

## Who they are

Independent tour operators running guided experiences. 1–10 staff. Experiences include walking tours, adventure activities, cultural tours, specialist itineraries, multi-day trips. Often the founder is also the lead guide. Annual revenue £200k–£3m.

[Source: Brand_Context.md]

## What they spend on marketing

£1,000–£8,000/month. Often heavily reliant on GetYourGuide and/or Viator for discovery. Website exists but rarely converts. [Source: Brand_Context.md]

## How they get bookings now

GetYourGuide (20–30% commission — unverified, do not use as hard stat), Viator (27% — unverified), Tripadvisor Experiences, some direct. Word of mouth and repeat. [Source: Brand_Context.md — commission rates unverified, see unverified-claims.md]

## Their six pain points

| # | Pain point | Booked Wild angle |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Not appearing when travellers ask ChatGPT "best kayaking tour in Cornwall" or "guided foraging walk in the Lake District" | AI visibility fix — entity-dense copy, schema, llms.txt |
| 2 | Paying 20–30% commission to GetYourGuide or Viator on every booking | This is the sharpest commission pain point in the Booked Wild ICP — use carefully until rates are verified |
| 3 | A website that lists the tours but doesn't book them — visitors enquire, then book via OTA | Direct booking flow rebuild — the website must close, not just inform |
| 4 | Reviews on Tripadvisor Experiences that the platform owns — not portable to a direct booking path | Reviews as direct booking trust signals — BrightLocal data applies here |
| 5 | Every guest relationship is owned by the OTA — no email, no follow-up, no repeat booking channel | Direct Booking OS — guest data ownership, automated review requests |
| 6 | If GetYourGuide changes its algorithm or commission structure, bookings disappear overnight | Rented land / resilience — own your customer relationship |
