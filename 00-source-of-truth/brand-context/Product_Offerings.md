# Core Offerings

## Primary Product / Service Ladder

Booked Wild operates a productised offer with three commercial paths (Sprint, Programme, Flagship), three optional add-ons (Direct Booking OS, Hosting and Maintenance, AI Build), fed by a two-step audit funnel.

### Audit funnel (top of pipeline)

**Foundations Audit** — `/audit`
Free, automated. Branded white-label widget embedded on bookedwild.com. Operator enters their URL; we grade the five things Google and AI engines read first — structure, links, usability, performance, and social. Overall mark, category breakdowns, prioritised fixes. Delivered to inbox in two minutes. Captures email, business name, business URL into Supabase. Cost: £0.

**Deep Insight Audit** — `/audit/deep-insight`
A 12-page written report covering AI visibility across ChatGPT, Claude, and Google AI Overviews; Tripadvisor and Google gap analysis; booking-flow drop-offs surveyed screen by screen; and two-year OTA dependency arithmetic with named figures. Cost: £97. Delivered within 5 working days.

### Three commercial paths (the ladder)

Each path is a different speed and depth into the same Booked Wild Method. The audit tells the operator which door fits where their business actually is.

**Sprint** — `/services/sprint`
A 30-day focused engagement for solo operators and small experience providers running one cabin, one tour, or one venue. Scope: AI visibility, schema, off-site citations that AI engines pull from. Optional Solo Site rebuild bundled if the existing site is the bottleneck. Rolls in the scope of the legacy AI Visibility Fix and Tripadvisor + GBP Optimisation work. Cost: £1,500 standalone; £3,000 with Solo Site.

**Programme** — `/services/programme`
A 12-month partnership for independent properties and boutique operators with multiple bookable units, real revenue, and OTA dependency to reduce. Scope: AI visibility, monthly reporting, ongoing optimisation, and an Operator Site rebuild when the existing site becomes the ceiling on the gains already proved. Rolls in the scope of the legacy AI Visibility Fix, Tripadvisor + GBP Optimisation, and Embedded Itinerary work. Cost: £1,800/mo on the partnership; £4,250 for the Operator Site rebuild.

**Flagship** — `/services/flagship`
A 12-month engagement for groups, collectives, and destination brands running multiple properties or a destination marketing organisation. Scope: all five surfaces of the Booked Wild Method (Pitch, Publish, Product, Profile, Partnership) — including a Flagship-tier website rebuild. Six places per year, by application. Cost: £30,000 fixed.

### Add to any path

Optional layers that work alongside Sprint, Programme, or Flagship.

**Direct Booking OS** — `/services/direct-booking-os`
A CRM and marketing-automation platform built on GoHighLevel, configured for European travel and hospitality. Sits on top of Bokun, HostAway, Lodgify, or whatever booking system the operator already runs. Provides the lifecycle layer those platforms don't: pipeline tracker, workflows, funnel pages, unified inbox, lead capture, attribution. Two tiers:

- Self-Serve: £49/mo + £149 setup
- Done-for-You: £199/mo + £499 setup

**Hosting and Maintenance** — `/services/hosting`
Managed hosting on Astro or WordPress with monthly schema audits, content freshness checks, citation surface re-engineering, and proactive monitoring. Plugins, performance tools, and security updates included — no add-on invoices, no plugin licence surprises. Cost: £85/mo. Three months free with any website build.

**AI Build** — `/services/ai-build`
Two paths, scoped to the operator's operation.

- **Agents** — built inside Direct Booking OS for use cases including first-line replies, pre-arrival itineraries, message triage, abandoned-booking follow-up, and review-response drafting for human approval. Cost: £195 setup + £99–£249/mo per agent. Done-for-You Direct Booking OS tier required.
- **Claude Code** — Anthropic's coding agent set up on the operator's machine so they can build their own integrations and automations. Cost: £495 setup + optional £85/mo support.

# Value Proposition

## Core Promise

We get independent travel and hospitality operators recommended by AI search engines like ChatGPT and Perplexity, then turn that recommendation into direct bookings — proving every step with citation screenshots, posted prices, and quarterly research that anyone can verify.

For owner-operators who don't have time to manage marketing themselves, we run the entire visibility flywheel as a productised retainer.

## Key Benefits

1. **Visibility in AI search.** When travellers ask AI engines for recommendations in your category and region, you appear in the answer. Measured, tracked, and proved with screenshots.
2. **Direct bookings recovered from OTAs.** Every commission percentage point recovered from Booking.com, GetYourGuide, Viator, Airbnb, or Tripadvisor Experiences is margin returned to the operator's P&L.
3. **A website that AI engines and travellers both trust.** Schema-marked, fast-loading, structurally sound, with citation surfaces engineered for AI extraction and clear booking flows for human visitors.
4. **Productised services with posted prices.** No vague retainers, no "contact us for a custom quote", no surprise invoices. Fixed scope, fixed price, fixed timeline.
5. **AI agents handling repetitive work.** Inquiry response, review responses, pre-arrival itineraries, abandoned-booking recovery — all running on the £65/mo platform without additional fees.
6. **A founder and team who actually understand independent travel.** Colin's 15-year track record at Nivo Digital, his current embedded research project in Levi Finland, and the team's experience across European travel destinations make every conversation peer-to-peer rather than agency-sells-to-confused-operator.
7. **Public proof of the work.** Case studies with citation screenshots. Quarterly published research. The agency's own AI visibility tracked openly. No promises that can't be verified.

## Why Now

AI-mediated travel discovery has accelerated through 2025 and 2026. Travellers are increasingly asking ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews for recommendations rather than browsing OTAs or running Google searches. The structural shift from search-engine-mediated to AI-mediated discovery is already underway.

The window for independent operators to build AI citation share is open now and closing fast. AI engines develop persistence bias — once a brand is consistently cited, that pattern reinforces. Operators who establish AI visibility through 2026 will benefit from compounding citation patterns; operators who delay will face calcified competitive positions where competitors own the recommendation slots.

OTA commission rates have also continued to climb, with GetYourGuide reportedly increasing rates for some operators in 2025 and Viator's basic rate at 27%. The cost of platform dependency is rising at the same time the discovery channel is shifting. Operators who don't build direct booking infrastructure now face both a margin squeeze and a visibility crisis.

This is a one-shot moment. Booked Wild is positioned to capture the operators who recognise the shift and want to act on it.

## Common Objections

1. **"Can you guarantee ChatGPT will recommend us?"** — No responsible agency can guarantee that. What we can do is identify the specific structural signals AI engines use, implement the technical fixes, and build the citation surfaces that increase the likelihood of being recommended. We track and report on AI citation share monthly and report against agreed quarterly targets, so improvements (or lack of them) are visible in writing.

2. **"Isn't this just SEO with a new name?"** — No. Traditional SEO focuses on Google rankings and organic traffic. AI search optimisation works on different signals: schema, structured data, citation blocks, entity density, llms.txt, third-party validation, review velocity, and AI engine extraction patterns. Strong technical SEO is the foundation, but the citation flywheel runs on different fuel. Traditional SEO is a subset of one of the five surfaces we run inside the AI Visibility Service.

3. **"Why pay £97 for an audit when the Foundations Audit is free?"** — The free Foundations Audit grades five technical areas in two minutes: structure, links, usability, performance, social. The £97 Deep Insight Audit is twelve pages of human work covering AI visibility across ChatGPT, Claude, and Google AI Overviews; Tripadvisor and Google gap analysis; booking-flow drop-offs surveyed screen by screen; and two-year OTA dependency arithmetic. It's the difference between a structural baseline and an actionable strategy specific to the business.

4. **"We've been burned by agencies before."** — Most agencies sell open-ended retainers with vague deliverables. Booked Wild sells productised services with fixed scope, fixed price, fixed timeline. Even our flagship recurring service — the AI Visibility Service — is fully scoped in writing with quarterly outcome targets. You see what you're getting and what it costs before you commit. Our case studies include the specific dates work was delivered and the specific outcomes measured.

5. **"Can't I just ask ChatGPT for AI visibility advice myself?"** — You can get general guidance. What you can't do without specialist knowledge is implement the technical layer (schema, llms.txt, citation block formatting), audit your competitor citation share, build the third-party validation signals (Tripadvisor, GBP, review systems), produce the editorial cadence AI engines reward, run partnership outreach, and operate AI agents handling enquiries. The strategy might be available to anyone; the execution at scale is what we sell.

6. **"Why should we trust an agency in Bristol and Finland to understand our region?"** — We launch destination by destination, working with operators in clusters per region. Within 6 months of launching in your destination, we typically have 10+ operators in our pipeline, real local case studies, and named relationships with the regional businesses that matter. We're not pretending to be everywhere; we go deep in specific regions.

7. **"I don't have time for another marketing project."** — That's exactly who the AI Visibility Service is built for. Onboarding takes 90 minutes. After that, most clients spend around 30 minutes per month on this work. We ship for review, not specs from scratch. If you don't reply within 5 working days, we publish anyway — silence becomes a feature, not a bottleneck.

8. **"What's the difference between £495 AI Visibility Fix and £1,500/mo AI Visibility Service?"** — The £495 Fix is a one-off implementation: schema, llms.txt, citation formatting, FAQ schema. Once delivered, you maintain it yourself. The £1,500/mo Service is the ongoing flywheel: the Fix is included in month one, plus we run Pitch, Publish, Product, Profile, and Partnership monthly with all four AI agents bundled. Buy the Fix if you have an internal team to maintain it. Buy the Service if you're an owner-operator who doesn't have time.
