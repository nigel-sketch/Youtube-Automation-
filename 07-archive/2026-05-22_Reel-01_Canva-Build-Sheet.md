## After you've built the 7 pages

1. **Add the voiceover.** Record in Canva (mic icon → Record yourself) or upload an MP3 from ElevenLabs / your VO artist. Drag it onto the audio track. Snap each phrase to the timestamp in the table.
2. **Add a music bed.** Canva → Audio → search "ambient cinematic" or "dark minimal". Filter by 30–45 s. Drag onto track 2. Right-click → set volume to 18 %.
3. **Burn in the captions.** For TikTok especially. Add each "Caption / subtitle line" as a separate text element at the bottom of every scene: 56 px Bold, off-white, on a dark rectangle at 70 % opacity. Anchor 280 px from the bottom (Reels safe area).
4. **Set the cover.** Timeline → click scene 7 → "Set as cover" (or screenshot frame at 00:36 and use as thumbnail).
5. **Export.** Share → Download → MP4 video, Quality 1080p, all pages selected.

## Safe-area check (do this before exporting)

- Nothing important within 200 px of the top edge or 320 px of the bottom edge.
- Symbol badge in scenes 2–3 sits at top-left, 80 px from the corner — OK on Reels and TikTok.
- End-card URL is in the middle band (between 35 % and 60 % vertical). Survives every platform overlay.

## What not to add

- No photos. No video clips. No icons except the small amber squares used as bullets.
- No emoji. No GIFs. No Canva "AI image" or "Magic Media" output.
- No glowing tech visuals, robots, brain graphics, circuit lines, or generic technology imagery.
- Only the three brand assets in this folder, plus type, plus the brand colours.

## If something doesn't look right

- Text feels small on phone preview → bump headline sizes by 10 px.
- Motion feels jerky → set every animation to "Slow" speed in the animation panel.
- Contour texture too loud → drop opacity to 25 %.
- Wordmark looks crushed on dark → check you're using `logo-wordmark-on-dark.png`, not `logo-wordmark.png`.
