# Reel 01 — Invisible at the decision point

**Format:** Brand-led motion graphic, 9:16 vertical (1080×1920) master, with 1:1 (1080×1080) and 4:5 (1080×1350) crops for LinkedIn.
**Length:** 00:40 (target).
**Voice mode:** Anti-OTA Advocate, with a Commercial Guide close.
**Channels:** Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn.
**Source assets (this folder only):** `logo-symbol.png`, `logo-wordmark.png`, `logo-wordmark-on-dark.png`, `contour-texture.svg`.
**Palette:** background `#070A0F`; amber `#BB682E`; off-white text sampled from the `logo-wordmark-on-dark.png` mark (≈ `#F2EBDD`).
**Type:** wordmark for the brand only; on-screen copy uses the closest sans paired with the wordmark — Inter Tight or Söhne, weights 600/700, tracking −10, line-height 1.05.
**Music:** instrumental bed, sub-driven, 90–100 BPM, no melody hooks. Light tape hiss + low rumble. Single low brass sting on the wordmark reveal (Shot 4).
**Sound design:** typewriter-style tick on each engine name; soft amber whoosh on text wipes; UI-confirm tick on each audit-checklist item; muted thump on end-card lockup.

**Aspect ratio crops.**
- 9:16 master (Reels, TikTok, Shorts).
- 1:1 crop for LinkedIn feed: keep all centred elements, lift wordmark/badge by 8 % vertically.
- 4:5 crop for LinkedIn alt: same as master, trim 18 % off bottom contour bleed.

**Safe-area rules.** Keep all text inside a 12 % vertical inset top and bottom (Reels UI eats the bottom 16 %; TikTok eats the top right and bottom). End-card URL must sit between 35 % and 60 % vertical to survive every crop and platform overlay.

**Export specs.**
- Master: H.264, 1080×1920, 30 fps, 12 Mbps, AAC stereo 320 kbps, −14 LUFS, true peak −1 dBTP.
- Captions burnt-in on a TikTok cut for sound-off viewers (use the subtitle text below verbatim).
- Cover frame: 00:36.0 (end-card lockup).

Booked Wild checks whether AI engines can read the things they need to read to recommend you — your website, your reviews, your prices, your Google Business Profile and your booking flow.

Run the free Foundations Audit and see exactly where your business disappears.
→ bookedwild.com/audit

## Brand-rules check

- No banned words (`help`, `unlock`, `solutions`, `bespoke`, `transform`, `leverage`, `seamless`, `cutting-edge`, etc.) in any deliverable.
- No "from £" / "starting at" pricing language; the audit is named as free, which is its actual price.
- No stock imagery, no robots, no glowing brains, no circuits — visuals are 100 % brand assets from this folder plus type and motion.
- Active CTA verb ("Run the free Foundations Audit"), not a placeholder.
- Voice mode is consistent within the piece (Anti-OTA Advocate body, Commercial Guide CTA), as the Voice Guide allows.
- UK English throughout (travellers, optimisation, recognise, business profile).
- Entity density is high in the caption: ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Booked Wild, Foundations Audit, Google Business Profile, Europe.
