# Social Content QA Checklist

Before sending to Colin, check:

- Is the target ICP clear?
- Is the pain point specific?
- Is the hook strong enough?
- Is the post useful, not generic?
- Is there a credible source or data point?
- Is the claim supported?
- Does it sound human?
- Does it avoid over-polished AI tone?
- Is it practical for Colin to film?
- Is the channel fit clear?
- Is the CTA or next step clear?
- Would this build trust with a travel or hospitality business owner?
