## FindQuestions.com — tool note (added 2026-05-23)

FindQuestions.com outputs are AI-generated topic suggestions based on publicly available Reddit discussions. They are useful for ideation, question discovery and audience-language research, but should not be used as evidence of search volume, market demand or factual claims without separate verification.

The PDF disclaimer states the suggestions are provided as-is and are not guaranteed to drive traffic or rankings. Source: blog-topics-direct-bookings-for-holiday-re.pdf, reviewed 2026-05-23.

All FindQuestions.com outputs are stored in 03-research/question-bank.md under the label "FindQuestions.com PDF" and are tagged for separate verification before any are used in publishable content.
