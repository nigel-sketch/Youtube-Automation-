# Question Bank

## Purpose

Store audience questions, Reddit-derived prompts, FAQ ideas and content angles for Booked Wild.

Questions in this file are not factual claims. They are prompts for content research, SEO/AEO planning, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn posts and direct-booking website improvements.

## Source rules

- Questions from FindQuestions.com are ideation inputs only.
- Do not treat them as verified claims.
- Do not cite them as evidence of search volume or audience scale.
- Verify any factual claim separately before using in public content.
